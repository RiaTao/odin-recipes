Blueberry Muffins

Description

Out of butter but still craving some blueberry muffins? This recipe has got your back! As always make sure to skim the recipe before you start. Happy Baking!

* this recipe is modified from the original

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder, packed
  • 1/3 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup Apple Sauce, melted
  • 6 tbsp White Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 2/3 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 3/4 cup Frozen Blueberries

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl Whisk together apple sauce and sugar. Add eggs one at a time, beat until well incorporated.
  4. Mix in vanilla extract.
  5. Alternated adding flour and milk, beat at low speed to combine. The batter should be very thick.
  6. Fold the blueberris into the batter, careful not to overmix.
  7. Pour batter into prepared muffin tin.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes, until lightly golden, let cool for about 10 minutes before removing.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This modified recipe can make just as chewy chocolate chip cookies as the original* for less sugar and butter! As always make sure to skim the recipe before you start. Happy Baking!

* this recipe is modified from the original
  • 1/3 cup White Granulated Sugar
  • 1/3 cup Light Brown Sugar, packed
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup Unsalted Butter, melted
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 and 1/8 cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 3.5 oz Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chunks

Steps

  1. Whisk together the sugars, salt, and putter until lumps are gone.
  2. Whisk in the egg and vanilla, until ribbons fall off the whisk and remain for a short while before falling back into the mixture.
  3. Fold in chocolate chunks, and chill dough for atleast 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F, and line a baking sheet with parchement paper
  5. Form 12 dough balls and place in evenly spaced rows on the baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
